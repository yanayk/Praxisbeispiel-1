About us

We are a small, but nonetheless strong team of Bebooks. What's Bebook? A social medium where users can view different genres of books and get detailed information about a book, such as author, publication date, reviews. These can be visualised both by regular reviews (textual by ordinary people, as well as by (if available) video (YouTube, reels). In addition, you can also (with the consent of the users of course) create a map and see where and by whom this book is read.

Bebook is registered under the following international classes to ensure full legal protection of our software services:
1.Class 9: Downloadable software applications for mobile devices and computers; downloadable digital books.

Legal Clearance & Brand Safety

We ensured that our brand did not infringe on existing trademarks by adhering to the following:
1. Word Choice: "Bebook" is a distinct, neological brand name. A search in TMview and EUIPO confirms no identical trademarks in the software or social media sectors in Austria.
2.Visual Integrity: All icons and graphics are original or used under strict license from Icons8, Freepik, and Unsplash, with appropriate credits documented in our index.html(main page) and license page
3.Positioning: Our layout and brand placement are unique to our specific "reader map" and "video review" niche, distinguishing us from general competitors like Goodreads or Amazon.
